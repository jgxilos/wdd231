DentalCare+

Individual Project Site Plan (WDD 231)

1. Site Name

DentalCare+

Rationale: This name conveys professionalism, trust, and health. It reflects the purpose of offering a modern, human, and accessible dental experience focused on patient well-being and high-quality services.

Suggested Domain (Optional): dentalcareplus.com

2. Site Purpose

The main purpose of DentalCare+ is to offer an informative, accessible, and visually modern website for patients seeking reliable dental care. The site will serve as a central hub to:

  • Provide a clear presentation of the clinic, its mission, and its values.
  • Detail the comprehensive catalog of dental services offered (cleanings, orthodontics, implants, whitening, etc.).
  • Facilitate online appointment requests via a functional, validated form.
  • Display complete contact information, hours, and location with an interactive map.
  • Build confidence and credibility through patient testimonials.

3. Scenarios (Target Audience Questions)

These are typical questions a site visitor might ask, which drive the content development:

  1. “What dental services does this clinic offer, and which one is right for me?” (Requires a detailed Service section with clear descriptions.)
  2. “Where is the clinic located, and how can I contact them immediately?” (Requires complete Contact details, hours, and an embedded map.)
  3. “Can I request an appointment online quickly and securely?” (Requires a simple, functional, and validated Appointment Form.)
  4. “What do other patients say about the quality of treatment received?” (Requires a Testimonials section to build trust.)

4. Color Scheme

The chosen palette is based on colors that convey cleanliness, health, and confidence. They will be used consistently across the site.

Primary Colors

#61BACA (Primary Brand Color) | Usage: Header Background, Primary Buttons ("Book Appointment"), Primary Icons, Main Links.
#1D837F (Primary Dark / Accent) | Usage: Button Hover State, Strong Separators, Footer Background, Main Headings (H1, H2).

Supporting Colors

#3EAEB1 (Secondary Visual Element) | Usage: Service Card Icons, Sub-headings (H3), Decorative Underlines.
#9FD8E1 (Soft Accent) | Usage: Soft Backgrounds for alternate sections (e.g., Testimonials, About Us).

Neutral Colors

#9CD1CE (UI Neutral) | Usage: Soft borders, Wireframe background, Subtle background in forms.
#D7EAEE (Main Background) | Usage: Global page background for most sections.

5. Typography

The font selection balances professionalism, clarity, and modern aesthetics.

  • Headings (H1, H2, H3): Montserrat (Sans-serif). Transmits cleanliness, professionalism, and clear visual hierarchy.
  • Body Text (Paragraphs, Lists): Lato (Sans-serif). Chosen for high legibility on digital screens, providing a feeling of clarity and accessibility.
  • Highlighted Elements (Quotes, CTAs): *Playfair Display (Serif)*. (This font is optional for special sections like Testimonials, adding a touch of confidence/formality).
