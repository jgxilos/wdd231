Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I visit the dentist?

We recommend visiting the dentist every 6 months for routine cleanings and check-ups. However, some patients may need more frequent visits depending on their oral health needs.

Do you offer emergency dental services?

Yes! We provide same-day emergency appointments for urgent dental issues like severe pain, broken teeth, or dental trauma. Call us immediately if you have a dental emergency.

What should I expect during my first visit?

Your first visit includes a comprehensive examination, X-rays, oral cancer screening, and a discussion of your dental health goals. We'll create a personalized treatment plan if any issues are found.

Are cosmetic procedures covered by insurance?

Most cosmetic procedures are not covered by insurance as they're considered elective. However, some treatments may be partially covered if they also serve a restorative purpose. We can help you understand your coverage.

How long does teeth whitening last?

Professional teeth whitening typically lasts 1-3 years, depending on your lifestyle habits. Avoiding staining foods/drinks and maintaining good oral hygiene can help extend the results.
